# Vox Script — WhatsApp Voice Note

**Duration:** ~5-6 minutes
**Tone:** Personal, informal, like a friend explaining an opportunity over the phone
**Language:** Roman Urdu (Pakistani) — polished but conversational, no Hindi vocabulary

---

## Intro (30 sec)

Assalam-o-Alaikum.

Mera naam [aap ka naam] hai aur main German Care Academy se baat kar raha hoon.

Aap ne shayad woh banner dekha hoga — **"Chhoti si salary par guzara kyun?"**

To yahi wajah hai ke main aap ko ye voice note bhej raha hoon. Main chahta hoon ke aap ko ek aise mauqe ke baare mein bataun jo aap ki zindagi badal sakta hai.

Aur main wada karta hoon — ye koi sales pitch nahi hai. Bas ek seedhi saadhi baat hai.

---

## The Problem (45 sec)

Aao sach sach baat karte hain.

Aaj kal Pakistan mein naukri milna kitna mushkil ho gaya hai, aap khud jaante hain. Aur agar mil bhi jaye toh 25-40 hazar mein ghar chalana — mahina khatam hone se pehle paisa khatam.

Kuch bachta nahi. Koi savings nahi. Koi future planning nahi.

Aur sab se badi baat — koi guarantee nahi ke kal ko naukri rahegi bhi ya nahi.

Ye aap ka qusoor nahi hai. Ye system hai. Lekin ek raasta aur bhi hai.

---

## The Opportunity (1 min)

Aur woh raasta hai — **Germany ka Ausbildung program.**

Ye koi VIP program nahi hai aur na hi koi scheme. Ye Germany ka sarkaari taleem-o-tarbiyat ka nizaam hai. Aur is mein daakhila lene ke liye aap ko laakhon rupaye bank mein block karne ki zaroorat nahi.

Suno — **Zero blocked account.**

German hukoomat aur hospital aap ki training ka poora kharcha uthate hain. Aap ko ek bhi rupiya fees mein nahi dena.

Aur haan — training ke dauran hi aap ko **maahana 4 lakh rupaye** tak stipend milta hai. Jee haan — parhte hue kamayein.

Program mukammal karne ke baad tankhwah aur bhi bohot zyada ho jaati hai.

Aur aap ko ek **sarkaari German contract** milta hai — job placement, training duration, stipend — sab kuch tehreeri taur par likha hota hai. Yeh European qualification hai, poori duniya mein valid hai.

---

## Objection Handling (1 min)

Aap soch rahe honge — **"Yeh sab theek hai lekin..."**

**"Mere paas itne paise nahi hain"** — to suniye, paise ki zaroorat hi nahi hai. Zero blocked account, zero fees. Aap khaali haath Germany ja sakte hain.

**"Fees afford nahi hongi"** — free education hai. German hukoomat aur hospital poora kharcha uthate hain. Aap ko kuch nahi dena.

**"Job guarantee kya hai?"** — aap ko ek official German contract milta hai. Yeh koi molvi kaati ka wada nahi hai. Qanooni dastaweez hai jis mein sab kuch likha hota hai.

**"Germany jaana bohot mushkil hoga"** — hum aap ko har step par guide karte hain. A1 se B2 tak language classes, visa documentation, hospital placement — sab kuch. Aap ko bas **German language seekhni hai**. Humare expert teachers aap ko train karein ge aur aap ka ye kaam bhi aasaan kar dein ge.

---

## Eligibility — Kya aap qualify karte hain? (45 sec)

Ab aap soch rahe honge — "Yeh sab theek hai, lekin kya main qualify karta hoon?"

Suniye, yeh koi difficult criterion nahi hai. Bahut simple hai.

**Age:** Aap ki umar 18 saal se 35 saal ke darmiyan hai — regardless of your age today, Germany mein nursing Ausbildung ke liye 35 tak koi masla nahi. Koi upper limit fixed nahi hai lekin 18-35 ideal range hai.

**Education:** Aap ne FSc, FA, ya A-Levels kiya hai — koi bhi stream, koi specific percentage ki zaroorat nahi. FSc Pre-Medical wale hain toh aur behtar, lekin agar aap ke paas 12 saal ki taleem hai toh aap apply kar sakte hain. Diploma holders bhi aa sakte hain.

**Language:** Aap ko German seekhni hai. B1 minimum, B2 recommended. Kya aap ko German aati hai? Abhi nahi — lekin isi liye toh hum 8 mahine ka intensive program offer kar rahe hain. A1 se B2 tak — hum aap ko tayyar karte hain.

**Aur kya chahiye?** Valid passport (kam se kam 1 saal ki validity), koi criminal record nahi hona chahiye, aur sehat theek honi chahiye. Bas.

Aur sab se important baat — **aap ke paas paise nahi hain to koi baat nahi.** Koi paisa block nahi karna. Koi fees nahi. Aap khaali haath bhi aa sakte hain.

---

## The Pathway (45 sec)

Ab suniye — yeh hai aap ka roadmap.

Yeh koi 5 saala program nahi hai.

**Sirf 8 mahine — A1 se B2 tak intensive German language course.**

Har level 2 mahine. Focused, intensive, result-oriented.

- Mahina 1-2: **A1 Intensive** — daily classes, native German teachers
- Mahina 3-4: **A2 Intensive** — speaking practice, rozmarra ki baatein
- Mahina 5-6: **B1 Intensive** — healthcare vocabulary, nursing terms shuru
- Mahina 7-8: **B2 Intensive** — professional proficiency, exam prep

**Aur phir — mahina 9+ — Germany.**

Paid Ausbildung shuru. 4 lakh rupaye maahana stipend. Aap ka career officially start.

---

## Your Choice (30 sec)

Aur ek baat — aap ko Germany mein nursing ka kaunsa field pasand hai?

Teen options hain, aur koi galat jawab nahi hai:

**Altenpflege** — boodhon ki dekhbhaal. Germany mein sab se zyada demand hai. Agar aap ko logon ki khidmat karna pasand hai to yeh aap ka field hai.

**Krankenpflege** — general nursing. Hospital, clinics. Sab se versatile option. Aage chal ke specialization ke bhi mauqe hain.

**Kinderkrankenpflege** — bachchon ki nursing. Pediatric hospitals. Agar bachchon ke saath kaam karna achha lagta hai.

Jo aap ko suit kare, woh chunein.

---

## CTA (45 sec)

To ab sawaal yeh hai — kya aap apni zindagi ka ek ahem faisla aaj karna chahain ge?

Yeh sirf ek information session nahi hai. Yeh aap ki zindagi ka turning point ho sakta hai.

Hum aap ko **muft 1-on-1 consultation** dete hain. Sirf 20 minute. Zero commitment.

Hum aap ki eligibility check karein ge, aap ke sawaalon ke jawab dein ge, aur aap ko ek clear roadmap dein ge.

**Aaj hi humein WhatsApp karein — 0323 1525249 — aur message mein "COFFEE" likhein.**

Ya phir link par click karein aur apni consultation book karwain.

Yaad rakhein — aaj se sirf **9+ mahinon** mein aap Germany mein ho sakte hain.

Farq sirf ek decision ka hai.

Shukriya. Allah Hafiz.

## Production Notes

- **Read slowly** with natural pauses at line breaks
- Use a warm, conversational tone — imagine talking to a younger cousin or friend
- **Bold phrases** should be emphasized with slightly slower delivery
- Total time: ~5-6 minutes
- If recording in parts: split after Objection Handling (Part 1) and start Pathway (Part 2)
