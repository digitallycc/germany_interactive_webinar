Age: 18-35 years
Minimum 18. Ideal 18-30. 35 tak possible for Healthcare & Nursing — Germany mein experience zyada important hai umar se.

Education: FSc / Intermediate
12 saal ki taleem (FSc/FA/A-Levels) kaafi hai. FSc Pre-Medical ko preference hai lekin sab streams welcome. Diploma holders bhi apply kar sakte hain.

German: B1-B2 required
B1 minimum visa ke liye. B2 recommended for healthcare. Goethe/telc/ÖSD accept. Hum aapko A1 se B2 tak 8 months mein tayyar karte hain.

Other Requirements
Valid passport (1 year+), clean criminal record, sehat theek. Koi paisa block nahi — bank balance na hona bhi koi masla nahi.
