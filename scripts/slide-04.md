# Slide 04 — Eligibility (Audio Script)

**Tone:** Encouraging, straightforward
**Duration:** ~50 sec

---

Ab aap soch rahe honge — "Yeh sab theek hai, lekin kya MAIN qualify karta hoon?"

Tou Suniye, yeh koi difficult criteria nahi hai. Bahut simple hai.

**Age:** Aap ki umar 18 se 35 saal ke darmiyan hai? Perfect. Healthcare & Nursing ke liye Germany mein 35 tak koi masla nahi.

**Education:** Aap ne FSc, FA, ya A-Levels kiya hai? 12 saal ki taleem kaafi hai. Koi specific percentage nahi chahiye. FSc Pre-Medical ho toh aur behtar, lekin sab streams welcome hain. Diploma holders bhi aa sakte hain.

**Language:** B1 minimum visa ke liye, B2 recommended healthcare ke liye. Kya aap ko German aati hai? Abhi nahi — lekin isi liye toh hum 8 mahine ka program offer kar rahe hain. A1 se B2 tak, hum aap ko tayyar karte hain.

**Aur kya chahiye?** Valid passport kam se kam 1 saal ki validity, clean criminal record, sehat theek. Bas.

Aur sab se important baat — aap ke paas paise nahi hain toh koi baat nahi. Koi paisa block nahi karna. Koi fees nahi.
