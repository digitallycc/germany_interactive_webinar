# Slide 03 — Objections Answered (Audio Script)

**Tone:** Reassuring, confident, countering doubts directly
**Duration:** ~55 sec

---

Ab aap ke zehen mein kuch sawaal zaroor aa rahe honge. Chaliye, main unka jawab deta hoon.

Pehla — _"Mere paas paise nahi hain."_ Toh suniye, paise ki zaroorat hi nahi hai. Study visa ke liye blocked account ki requirement nahi hai. Aap khaali haath aa sakte hain.

Doosra — _"Fees afford nahi hogi."_ Free education hai. German government aur hospital poora kharcha utha rahe hain. Aap ko ek bhi rupiya nahi dena. Aur parhte hue Rs. 4 lakh per month stipend milega — earn while you learn.

Teesra — _"Job guarantee kya hai?"_ Yeh koi aam se banday ka wada nahi hai. Aap ko ek official German Ausbildung contract milta hai — legal document jismein stipend, training duration, sab kuch likha hota hai. European qualification hai, poori duniya mein valid.

Aur chautha — _"Germany jaana bohot mushkil hoga."_ Hum aap ko har step par guide karte hain. A1 se B2 tak language classes, visa documentation, hospital placement — sab kuch. Aap ko bas German seekhni hai. Humare expert teachers aap ko tayyar karte hain. Simple.

Zero risk. Sirf aap ka time aur mehnat chahiye.
