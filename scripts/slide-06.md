To ab sawaal yeh hai — kaise hoga yeh sab?

Yeh raha aap ka roadmap. Sirf 8 mahine. Phir Germany.

**Months 1-2 — A1 Intensive.** Regular classes, Expert German teachers. Koi pehle se German aana zaroori nahi hai. Bilkul zero se shuru.

**Months 3-4 — A2 Intensive.** Ab aap rozmarra ki baatein karne lagein ge. Speaking practice, confidence build. Aap express karna start karein ge.

**Months 5-6 — B1 Intensive.** Yahan se healthcare vocabulary aur nursing terms shuru. Hospital communication. Intermediate level.

**Months 7-8 — B2 Intensive.** Professional proficiency. Exam preparation. Nursing terminology complete. Yeh aap ka entry ticket to Germany hai.

**Month 9+ — Welcome to Germany!** Paid Ausbildung shuru. Rs. 4 lakh per month stipend. Aap ka career officially start.

Aaj join kiya, 8 months mein B2, phir Germany. Next batch July 2026 hai.

Time nikal raha hai — lekin aap ke liye ab bhi mauqa hai.
