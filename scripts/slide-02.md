# Slide 02 — Reality Check (Audio Script)

**Tone:** Truthful, empathetic, unemotional
**Duration:** ~50 sec

---

Achha, aao sach sach baat karte hain.

Aaj Pakistan mein naukri milna kitna mushkil hai, aap khud jaante hain. Agar mil bhi jaaye, toh 25-40 hazaar mein mahina kaise guzarta hai? Kuch bachta nahi. Koi savings nahi. Koi future planning nahi. Aur sab se badi baat — koi guarantee nahi ke kal ko naukri rahegi bhi.

Yeh aap ka qusoor nahi hai. Yeh system hai.

Lekin ek raasta aur bhi hai — Germany ka Ausbildung program. Yahan par aap ko Rs. 4 lakh per month stipend milta hai training ke dauran. Zero fees. Zero blocked account. Aap khaali haath bhi aa sakte hain. Aur aap ko ek official German contract milta hai — job guaranteed, sab kuch likha hota hai.

Dekhiye, farq sirf ek decision ka hai. Aaj Pakistan mein struggle... ya kal Germany mein secure life.

Aap ke liye kaunsa behtar hai?
